Student Activities and Leadership with the opportunity to recognize the unique accomplishments and dedication of UMass Boston's exemplary students. Our students manage to go beyond what is expected or required of them to participate in university activities, clubs, and organizations. This is your opportunity to recognize students for their outstanding accomplishments. Congratulations to this year’s Beacon Leadership Awards recipients and all those who were nominated for an award.
