At Camp Shriver, children with and without disabilities build social skills and positive social relationships by learning and playing together. In addition to our three community programs below, Camp Shriver has also launched a successful replication effort at Stonehill College. To learn more about Camp Shriver at Stonehill College or to request an application, visit the Stonehill College website or contact the Education Department at Stonehill College at 508.565.1553.
