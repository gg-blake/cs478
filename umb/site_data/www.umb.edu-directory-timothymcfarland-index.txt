McFarland Department: Performing Arts Title: Senior Lecturer II Location: McCormack Hall Floor 02 Phone: 617.287.6982 Send Email Areas of Expertise Piano, music theory and piano instruction Degrees MM, New England Conservatory Additional Information A former student of Russell Sherman, Mr. McFarland performs frequently in the Boston area as piano recitalist, soloist with orchestra and as chamber music collaborator. He has premiered works by Robert Ceeley, Peter Lieberson, David Patterson and Daniel Pinkham. Mr. McFarland serves as the Music Director of the Belmont Symphony Orchestra. He is a faculty member and former director of the New School of Music and is also an Affiliate Artist at MIT.
