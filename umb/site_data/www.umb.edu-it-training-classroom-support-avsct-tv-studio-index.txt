All Studio lighting is controlled by an ETC Dimming Control System. The studio is equipped with 2 Sony XDCAM Cameras with rear studio controls, 1 Sony DVCAM Camera with rear studio controls and 1 Sony HDV Z7U HD Camera. The studio is also equipped with a Q-TV Teleprompter System. The studio is connected to the Master Control room, which has; a TriCaster, a Mackie 16 channel audio console, an RTS Intercom System, and a Gentner telephone hybrid. We can master to DVCAM, DVCPRO, Beta SP or DVD formats. We also have a 10 Bay DVD duplication station.
