Faisal-Nichols Honorary Scholarship Chancellor Marcelo Suárez-Orozco has seeded an endowed scholarship to honor the lives of UMass Boston student Sayed Arif Faisal and Tyre Nichols by providing tuition support to talented students with financial need. Your help growing this transformational fund on behalf of UMass Boston students is greatly appreciated. "We must come together to deepen our commitment to learning and striving for change." - Chancellor Marcelo Suárez-Orozco It takes just 60 seconds to make a contribution .
