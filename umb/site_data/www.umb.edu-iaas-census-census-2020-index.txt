The Institute for Asian American Studies is posting selected data on Asian Americans and other racial/ethnic groups in Massachusetts from the 2020 Decennial Census on this page. Please note that data may refer to persons who identified themselves as one race “alone” or as one or more race(s), called “alone or in combination,” and the tables are labeled accordingly.
