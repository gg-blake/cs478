Gerontology Institute Title: Senior Research Fellow Send Email Areas of Expertise Tyler Compton joined the Pension Action Center from Vermont Legal Aid, where she worked in the Elder Law Unit on matters including housing preservation, Social Security appeals, Medicaid eligibility, debt collection defense, and aid to victims of financial exploitation and abuse. Tyler’s experience also includes an internship at PAC from 2015-16. She is a graduate of Boston College Law School and Smith College, and she is a member of the bars of Vermont and Massachusetts.
