for Archaeological Research The Andrew Fiske Memorial Center for Archaeological Research plays an integral role in the UMass Boston Historical Archaeology graduate program. Graduate students often participate in Center research projects for their Research Assistantships, and many use project sites and data in their master’s theses. Center Staff oversee research in Eastern North America, the American Southwest, Iceland, and Latin America. Stay up to date with Fiske Center projects with the Fiske Center Blog .
