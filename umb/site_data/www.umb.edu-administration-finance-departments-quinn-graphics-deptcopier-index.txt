Why centralized copier management? New installations are generally of Multi-Function Devices which can copy, print, fax, and scan. Participating departments pay a monthly base charge plus a per-copy charge. These charges cover the cost of the Lease Agreement, Maintenance Contract, and Toner. Quinn Graphics manages monthly chargebacks and, for most models, has the ability to break out copy usage costs to specified SpeedTypes and users. Quinn Graphics provides toner. Quinn Graphics offers technical Support. Quinn Graphics coordinates with ITS for the necessary network connections.
