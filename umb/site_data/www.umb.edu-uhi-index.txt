Join Email List LinkedIn Research & Impact UHI investigates and addresses the economic, social, and natural complexities found in coastal areas. Harbor Management Plans Working with the Towns of Nantucket, Mashpee, and Manchester-by-the-Sea to develop harbor management plans that are informed by science, policy, and public input. National Working Waterfront Network Leading several initiatives to advance working waterfronts in partnership with Network members. Municipal Technical Assistance Program Providing services to coastal municipalities seeking technical assistance on small projects such as mapping exercises, public engagement, and survey research. Visit the Urban Harbors  Institute Blog About Us Who We Are UHI works closely with governments, marine industry representatives, non-governmental organizations, and members of the public to understand and address a wide variety of ocean and coastal topics. Urban Harbors Institute Brochure (pdf) What We Do Provide research, technical assistance, and advisory services Urban Harbors Institute offers assistance in fields such as: Coastal and harbor planning Natural resource management Climate change Working waterfronts Coastal laws and regulations Environmental justice Community engagement and education Water transportation Geographical information systems Advance research and knowledge sharing UHI collaborates with partners to build networks dedicated to advancing thriving coastal communities and healthy ecosystems in Massachusetts and beyond. As a facilitator, manager, and convener of networks, webinars, and events, UHI aims to create space for productive conversations, information sharing, and capacity building. Educate the next generation of ocean and coastal professionals UHI provides UMass Boston’s diverse student body with hands-on experiences, preparing them for jobs after graduation. As employees, interns, volunteers, and program participants, students work on solving real-world problems while gaining important skills such as critical thinking, project management, and networking. Work With Us Many of our projects are conducted on behalf of and/or in partnership with others—including state/local/regional/federal governments, NGOs, other academic institutions, and environmental consulting companies.
