Such conduct is prohibited by federal law (Title IX) and by university policy. The Office of Civil Rights and Title IX is committed to working with all university community members to proactively address concerns of sexual and gender-based harassment through prompt addressing of concerns and complaints, remedial actions and prevention efforts. Faculty, staff, and student employees who know of or receive complaints of sexual misconduct should report these concerns or file a complaint with the university’s Title IX coordinator. If you, or someone you know, has been impacted by sexual or gender-based harassment, or you are someone involved in a formal complaint regarding sexual or gender-based harassment, you have the right to access resources and support.
