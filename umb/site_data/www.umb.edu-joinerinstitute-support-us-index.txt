To Donate Online Follow these instructions to make your tax deductible gift online! Go to the UMass Boston online giving page (link below) . Enter your donation amount Continue filling out all the required contact and billing information If you would like to designate your gift for a specific Research, Service or Celebration initiative (i.e. The Warrior Connection Residential Retreat, VSO Survey, BEACONS Project, Honor-A-Vet, etc.) please note that on the check, or include on a separate note. You will receive a confirmation email right away, and tax-deductible letter of receipt in the mail within 10 days. Thank you in advance!
