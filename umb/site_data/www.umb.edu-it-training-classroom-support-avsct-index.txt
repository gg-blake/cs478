Innovation About Information Technology Classroom Technology Support and AV Services Classroom Technology and AV Services provides services and access to equipment and facilities related to the use of instructional technology in the classroom. In addition, AV Services provides media support for non-classroom events such as functions, conferences, seminars, etc. The department also maintains and services 290 Technology Enhanced Classrooms (TECs), Conference Rooms , Labs, and Auditoriums on campus.
