Sexuality Studies Faculty & Staff Student Resources Research & Centers Dean's Office Welcome to the Department of Latin American & Iberian Studies The field of Latin American & Iberian studies explores the languages, literatures, and histories of Spain, Portugal, Latin America and the Caribbean, as well as the millions of people of Hispanic descent living in the United States. Request Info Apply Academic Programs Undergraduate Programs Latin American & Iberian Studies BA Latin American Studies Minor Portuguese Minor Spanish Language Minor Certificate Programs Spanish-English Translation (Certificate) About Us We prepare students for a broad spectrum of careers, we provide opportunities for students to acquire proficiency in Spanish or Portuguese, major in Latin American and Iberian Studie s , minor in Spanish Language , Portuguese Studies, or Latin American Studies , receive a certificate in Spanish-English Translation , or fulfill content area requirements for certification to teach Spanish in Massachusetts.
