We are here to provide you with information and guidance about general education curriculum and assessment, academic policy concerns, and waivers of deadlines and certain academic requirements. The UMass Boston faculty wants you to gain important knowledge, capabilities and attitudes through undergraduate coursework that gives you a foundation for lifelong learning. The general education program is structured to give you opportunities to: engage in critical reading and analysis understand and respect human diversity explore the principal approaches to knowledge learn in depth develop effective speaking, listening and writing skills reason logically and quantitatively work independently and collaboratively Quick Links Elementary Language Proficiency Application (BA students only): Instructions | Form Course Repeat Waivers (choose "Repeat Waiver Request Form" from list) Request for Credit Overload CM Students: Click here .
