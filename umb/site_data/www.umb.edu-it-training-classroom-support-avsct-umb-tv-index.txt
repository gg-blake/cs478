Security Research & Innovation About Information Technology Campus Digital Signage (UMBTV) AV Services and Classroom Technology Campus Digital Signage (UMBTV) can be used to advertise any upcoming UMB events, courses, campus information, etc. Still images and short videos (with no audio) can be played on the system. Specifications Resolution Size: 1920 x 1080 (Horizontal/Landscape Layout) Recommended Formats: JPEG, PDF, PNG, PowerPoint, Photoshop File, MP4 Please submit all requests to UMBTV@UMB.edu . When submitting your request, please include how long you would like the ad to run for (i.e. for the semester, until a specific date, ect.) You can always just send us information about an event in as text and we will create an ad based on that information.
