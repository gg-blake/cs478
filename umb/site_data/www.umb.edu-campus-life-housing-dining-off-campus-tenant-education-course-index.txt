This course is meant to give you the tools and resources needed to effectively complete your off-campus housing search. There are many things that you should know before beginning your search. Going through our course is a great way to: Learn effective housing search strategies Understand your rights as a tenant Resources the city and university have to offer in assisting you & more! Whether this is your first time searching for housing or you've done it a few times, there is still something that you can take away from this course. The course is worth 30 Beacon Reward Points . In order to get your points, you will need to complete the survey found at the end once all sections have been completed.
