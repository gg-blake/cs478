Hone your cultural and linguistic knowledge, launch your career. As a student in the French BA program at UMass Boston, you will become Immersed in the language and culture of France. With a focus on communication skills, cultural awareness, and critical thinking, this program provides you with the tools you need to navigate a globalized world with confidence. In this program, you will: Attain advanced proficiency in French language skills, including reading, writing, listening, and speaking Develop cultural competence through exploration of French and Francophone cultures, societies, and histories Hone your critical thinking skills by analyzing and interpreting literary, cultural, and political texts in French Career Possibilities Build a career as a language teacher in a public or private school and share your knowledge of French language and culture with students of all ages. Pursue opportunities in translation or interpretation, helping individuals and organizations communicate effectively across linguistic and cultural barriers. Or, explore careers in government and diplomacy, working for national or international organizations such as the United Nations, NATO, or various embassies and consulates. Whatever your goals, UMass Boston is here to support you. FRENCH 302 - Initiation a la litterature et a la civilisation francaises: Depuis le Moyen Age jusqu’a la fin du 1 3 Credit(s) FRENCH 303 - Intro Lit & Cult II 3 Credit(s) FRENCH 304 - French Conversation 3 Credit(s) FRENCH 307 - French and Francophone Popular Culture 3 Credit(s) FRENCH 310 - French for Heritage Speakers 3 Credit(s) FRENCH 312 - French in the Professional and Business World 3 Credit(s) French 400-Level Electives (6 Credits): Take two courses between French 411 and 498 (inclusive) or MLLC 480 at UMass Boston or in the Paris program. Additional French Electives (6 Credits); Take two courses between French 302 and 498 (inclusive) or MLLC 480 not already taken.
