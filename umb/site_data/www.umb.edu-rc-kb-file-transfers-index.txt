Linux and OS X: On a Linux or OS X system, its syntax is similar to the cp command, and is covered fully in the man pages ('man scp'). Windows: On a Microsoft windows system, we recommend (and support) the use of Cygwin/X .  Alternatively, there are various other scp clients available such as PuTTY or WinSCP .
