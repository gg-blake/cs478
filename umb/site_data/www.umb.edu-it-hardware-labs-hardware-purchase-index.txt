To accommodate periods of remote work, employees are very strongly urged to purchase laptops instead of desktops , which can be used to work from campus or from home. For productivity and ergonomic benefits, purchase a docking adapter to use a larger monitor, keyboard, and mouse instead of those built-in to your laptop. Apple (MacBook) Processor: M1 or latest Memory/RAM: 16GB Hard Drive: 512GB SSD Operating system: macOS 12.0 or greater Extended warranty is strongly recommended: AppleCare plus (4 years) Windows (Laptop) Processor: Intel Core i5 Gen10 or equivalent Memory/RAM: 16GB Hard Drive: 512GB SSD Operating system: Windows 10 or greater Extended warranty is strongly recommended: Prosupport Plus (4 years) Dell laptops are highly recommended when purchasing Windows-based computers as we have a Dell-certified technician in house for repair services. UMass Boston also offers students discounts on a wide range of computers, laptops, and other electronics through the UMass Dell Student Discounts program. Printers Office printers are centrally managed by Quinn Graphics. Please contact Quinn Graphics for all your office printing needs. Need More Help? If you need more help in selecting a computer, please contact us at our Getting Help page .
