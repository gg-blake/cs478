Trained student leaders serve to provide leadership development workshops for their peers and the UMass Boston community. GROW peer facilitators empower students to be leaders and agents of change on- and off-campus by providing student-led leadership and social justice workshops. Workshops Workshop topics include leadership styles, time management, public speaking, and more. Peer facilitators can work with your group to develop a workshop based on your needs.  Let us know your topic and potential time.
