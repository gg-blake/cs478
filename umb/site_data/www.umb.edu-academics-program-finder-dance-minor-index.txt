Minor Declare or Change Your Minor Request Info About UMass Boston Undergraduate Admissions Nurture Your Passion for Dance While Developing Foundational Techniques. Are you passionate about dance? UMass Boston’s Dance Minor is a fantastic opportunity to explore and expand your love for dance. As part of the Performing Arts Department , this program gives you a technical foundation in ballet, modern, jazz, and hip-hop dance techniques. You’ll also gain exposure and fundamental training in theater dance forms. The program’s dedicated faculty members facilitate performance and choreographic opportunities and encourage you to understand dance from historical and world perspectives. After completing the program, you’ll be equipped to perform in one or more areas of dance, develop and critically evaluate dance performances in different styles, and generally contribute to the world of dance. Prospective Undergraduate Students: Apply for admission to UMass Boston by completing an application.
