We support the academic success and personal development of our students by delivering exceptional care within welcoming and inclusive environments. Vision Our vision is to enrich higher education by promoting optimal health and well-being throughout the campus community. Values We uphold human dignity, social justice, and personal respect for all individuals. We affirm healthcare as a fundamental right. We prioritize prevention-based, student-centered health initiatives. We contribute to healthcare advancement through research, practice, and quality management. We maintain the highest standards of professional excellence, responsiveness, and ethical practice. We actively support the university's educational mission and embrace our integral role within it. Goals Deliver high-quality, accessible, and cost-effective healthcare and behavioral health services to the UMB campus community. Increase awareness of health promotion and disease prevention across both the campus and local community. Ensure physical spaces, layouts, medical and technological equipment, and environments support the latest healthcare, behavioral health, and educational service delivery. Foster integration of our services with other departments and programs that share common goals, objectives, and vision.
