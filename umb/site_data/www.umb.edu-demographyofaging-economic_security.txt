The Center looks at economic security through research and production of academic and white papers, community projects, and work with the City of Boston's Age Strong Commission. In addition, the center produces and maintains the Elder Index TM , a one-of-a-kind, county-by-county measure of the income needed by older adults to maintain independence and meet their daily living costs while staying in their own homes.
