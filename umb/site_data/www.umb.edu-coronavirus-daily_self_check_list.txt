If you reply YES to one or several of the questions below, STAY HOME and follow the steps below: Notify your supervisor. Consider getting a COVID-19 test. They are free and accessible to everyone on campus. On-campus COVID-19 testing is available by appointment only. Call 617-287-5660 to book an appointment. Or contact your health provider. Daily Self Checklist 1. Are you experiencing any of the following symptoms? Fever or chills (temperature over 100.3F) without having taken any fever reducing medications? Cough Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing Fatigue Muscle or body aches Headache New loss of taste or smell Sore throat Congestion or runny nose Nausea or vomiting Diarrhea 2. Have you or anyone you have been in close contact with been diagnosed with COVID-19, or been placed on quarantine for possible contact with COVID-19? 3. Have you been asked to self-isolate or quarantine by a medical professional or a local public health official? If you answered YES to any of the above questions, STAY HOME, contact your supervisor, and consider getting a COVID-19 test. We ask that employees use their best judgement when reviewing the questions. We understand that some individuals have chronic conditions, such as migraines or allergy-related coughs, that have symptoms captured on the checklist. If you have such a condition and feel that a YES response on the checklist is related to your chronic condition rather than COVID-19, you may come in but are advised to be surveillance tested regularly.
