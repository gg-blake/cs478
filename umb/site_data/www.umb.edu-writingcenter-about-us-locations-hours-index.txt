We're located at the end of this room. Once you come into room 1300, keep going until you reach a set of glass doors. Our location is past these glass doors. There are signs located throughout room 1300 to guide you. Satellite Location: We also have a satellite location in Wheatley Hall, sixth floor, room 48. We use the satellite for our evening hours. This location tends to be a bit quieter than our main location. Fall Hours Mondays: 10am-5:00pm (main location), 5:00pm-8:00pm (satellite location) Tuesdays: 10am-5:00pm (main location), 5:00pm-8:00pm (satellite location) Wednesdays: 10am-5:00pm (main location), 5:00pm-8:00pm (satellite location) Thursdays: 10am-5:00pm (main location), 5:00pm-8:00pm (satellite location) Fridays: 10am-2:00pm (main location) Sundays: 3:00pm-7:00pm (Online only) We have in person drop-in hours (no appointment needed) during the following days and times: Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays: 3-5pm (main location) Fridays: 12-2pm (main location) Note that drop-in hours sessions are usually shorter than our 45-minute scheduled appointments depending on consultant availability, number of writers waiting, and other factors.
