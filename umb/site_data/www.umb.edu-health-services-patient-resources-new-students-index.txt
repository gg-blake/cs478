Phone: 617-287-5660 Fax: 617-287-3977 Counseling Center 24/7 Crisis Phone Support 855.634.4135 Phone: 617.287.5690 Fax: 617.287.5507 counseling.center@umb.edu Health & Wellness wellness@umb.edu Information for New Students New Students & Families, Welcome to UMass Boston and University Health Services, your on-campus health center. We're here to support your health and well-being a you embark on your academic journey. New Student To-Do List: 1. Visit My Health Beacon Portal The My Health Beacon Portal is a secure system for scheduling/canceling appointments, messaging providers, uploading immunization records, and viewing immunization compliance status. 2. Review Your Portal and Update Your Information Review the information on the portal's homepage for important details about its functionalities. Select the "Profile" tab to ensure your information is accurate and up-to-date. You can also edit your phone number and select your phone carrier to receive text reminders for messages and appointments. 3. Submit Immunization Records and Tuberculosis Risk Assessment A majority of students are required to submit documentation of several vaccinations and screenings. All students are required to submit the TB Risk-Screening Form. The "IMM" registration hold is placed in early October (for fall) and in March (for spring), which indicates that Health Services does not have your up-to-date documentation on file. Resources: Immunizations & Screening Requirements page Instructions for Uploading Immunization Records My Health Beacon Portal For Questions: Contact the UHS General Medicine Clinic. Call (617) 287-5660. Send a secure message to a provider under the "Messages" tab on the health portal. Email uhs@umb.edu . 4. Consent to Treat Minor Patient Form (Students Under 18 Upon Arrival) MA law requires parental consent for the medical care of minors. If the student is under 18, complete the Consent to Treat Minor Form . Submission Options: Drop Off: UHS General Medicine Clinic (Quinn Building, 2nd Floor) Email: UHS@umb.edu . Fax : 617-287-3977 (Attn: Consent to Treat Minor Form) Mail : UHS General Medicine, 100 Morrissey Blvd., Boston, MA 02125. 5. Review Mandatory Health Insurance Confirm or waive the Student Health Insurance via WISER. Failure to act by the deadline results in automatic enrollment. Video: How to Waive Your Insurance For billing or waiver questions, contact the Bursar's Office at (617) 287-5350 or Bursar@umb.edu . For coverage and benefits questions, contact University Health Plans (UHP) at (833) 251-1125. 6. Bystander Intervention Training Module DEADLINE: October 1, 2024. UMass Boston provides mandatory training to equip students with skills to intervene in situations of harassment or sexual violence. All students must complete this training in their first semester regardless of their enrollment status or program type. You will receive an email from Get Inclusive ( umasseducation@education.umassp.edu) with a link to the training, which takes about 20-30 minutes to complete. Failure to complete this module by the October 1, 2024 deadline will result in a "WEH" registration hold being placed, blocking course registration. Once the module is completed, it can take up to 48 hours for the hold to be removed. *Please Note: This module will be made available for incoming Fall 2024 students during the week of September 9, 2024.* For Questions: For technical support, please contact support@getinclusive.com. For password reset or questions regarding hold removal after 48 hours of completion, please email uhs@umb.edu. IT Support For My Health Beacon issues, contact UHSITSupport@umb.edu . For WISER account or Microsoft Authenticator assistance, contact the IT Service Desk at (617) 287-5220.
