Recreation hires student employees to operate the facilities and programs in five different areas; Fitness and Wellness, Intramural Sports, Informal Recreation, Member Services, and Communication. Our student employees play a key role in providing the highest quality and most dependable service to the customers/participants. We are looking for the best students to fulfill a number of roles that share the same values that guide and govern us; Respect Positive attitude Integrity Professionalism Clear communication Continuous improvement Recreation positions and job descriptions.
