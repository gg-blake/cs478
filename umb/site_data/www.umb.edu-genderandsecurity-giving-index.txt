By supporting the consortium, you can help to: Bring sophisticated, multidimensional feminist gender analysis to the most intractable problems of war and structural violence; Create new ways for field-based practitioners, activists, researchers, and policymakers to teach and learn from each other; Provide resources for educators who are creating a new generation of researchers committed to advancing our understandings of gender, armed conflict and peacebuilding; Support the dedicated interns who come to work with the consortium from countries around the world (52 countries so far!) We invite you to support the Consortium on Gender, Security and Human Rights by making a tax-deductible contribution today.
