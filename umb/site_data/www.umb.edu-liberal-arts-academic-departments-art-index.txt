Dean's Office Welcome to the Department of Art & Art History The Art & Art History Department offers an array of courses in the history of Western and East Asian art and architecture, as well as studio courses in video, digital imaging, graphic design, sculpture, fiber, printmaking, photography, and painting. Students have the opportunity to develop their talents and learn techniques to create art as well as acquiring the critical skills to understand visual culture in its historical context.
