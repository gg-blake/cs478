University of Massachusetts Boston is committed to the health, safety and well-being of minors involved in programs on campus. The Protection of Minors Policy at the University of Massachusetts Boston establishes the responsibilities of Program Directors/Staff supervising minors when on the University campus or while participating in University activities off campus. This policy applies to University activities involving minors and programs for minors planned collaboratively with non-University organizations that operate in University facilities.
