Department: Gerontology Institute Title: Researcher Location: Wheatley Hall Floor 03 Phone: 617.287.7332 Send Email Additional Information Anna oversees all aspects of PAC’s operations, and also maintains a caseload of pension cases. Prior to joining PAC, Anna served as a Deputy Fair Lending Director at the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, where she worked to prevent illegal discrimination in lending. She also served previously as an attorney at the Federal Reserve Board; as an Assistant Attorney General at the Office of the Massachusetts Attorney General; and as a Special Assistant at the U.S. Department of Treasury. She earned her J.D. at Harvard Law School, and earned an M.Sc. in Economics from the London School of Economics. In addition to her role at PAC, she is an Adjunct Professor of Law at the University of Massachusetts Law School.
