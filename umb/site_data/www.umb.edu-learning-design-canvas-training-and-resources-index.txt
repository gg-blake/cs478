Once you log in to your account with your UMB email credentials, you will find the "Sail with Canvas" introductory course to help you learn the basic functionality, a sandbox/development shell to practice with Canvas, and any courses you are scheduled to teach through WISER. More training resources are listed below. Access Canvas Canvas Access URL Courses scheduled in WISER will automatically appear in Canvas. Faculty who have chosen to teach with Canvas (and their students) can access Canvas using UMass Boston credentials at: https://umassboston.instructure.com Introductory Training Sessions To help faculty get up to speed with Canvas, several online training sessions were offered by Canvas representatives. The first session recordings are available below and can be useful for learning Canvas alongside other resources: Part I: Canvas Introduction and Course Set-Up (4/17/2024) Part II: Course Flow and Content Creation (4/19/2024) Part III: Assignments and Grading (4/24/2024) Setting Up Your Canvas Course (7/17/2024) Assessments, Assignments, and Grading (7/19/2024) How-to Resources Following are additional resources to help with the transition to Canvas: Get Started with Canvas – Guide for Faculty Blackboard to Canvas Migration Resources Canvas Resources for Instructors (Video Clips) Student Resources Please find a collection of resources to share with students on the Student Resources page.
