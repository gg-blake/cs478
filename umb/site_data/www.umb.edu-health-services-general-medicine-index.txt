Welcome to UHS General Medicine at UMass Boston! Our comprehensive medical services cater to enrolled students' primary and episodic care needs. Whether you're managing ongoing health concerns or seeking evaluation for new symptoms, our team offers same-day appointments for episodic illnesses or injuries. Comprehensive Care for All Your Needs At UHS, our board-certified healthcare providers specialize in college health and handle a wide range of conditions, including: Infections like sore throats, colds, flu, and urinary tract infections Injuries such as sprains, wounds, concussions, and lacerations Symptoms like abdominal pain, fatigue, cough, and dizziness Chronic conditions such as asthma, migraines, and ADHD Skin issues including acne, rashes, and insect bites Musculoskeletal problems such as back pain and joint pain Procedures like ear irrigations, dressing changes, and wart treatment Sports physicals and evaluations for sports injuries Sexual health services To ensure effective communication and high-quality care for all students, we utilize Language Line translation services during appointments. This service provides real-time translation in over 240 languages, allowing non-English speaking students to receive the medical attention they need comfortably and accurately.
