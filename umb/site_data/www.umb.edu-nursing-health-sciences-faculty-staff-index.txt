Faculty members are also dedicated teachers who value the close working and mentoring relationships they build with students. Among the Exercise and Health Sciences faculty are Fellows of the American College of Sports Medicine and The Obesity Society, while Nursing faculty are recognized as Fellows of the Academy of Nursing Education, American Academy of Nursing, American Heart Association, Society of Behavioral Medicine, Academy of Behavioral Medicine Research and the American Academy of Nurse Practitioners. Additionally, many nursing faculty are members of The Sigma Theta Tau International Honor Society of Nursing. The strength of our academic offerings and the depth of our clinical/practical collaborations comes from how well-respected our faculty is within the health and fitness professions.
