To turn your computer in to IT, use the following instructions: Download the PRO-01-IT Equipment Data & Department Removal Form and fill it out. Email that file to ITServiceDesk@umb.edu to start the process with IT. The original Excel file must be emailed, no scanning, handwritten, photocopy, etc. Print out the form & adhere it to the surplus units. Bring the surplus computers to IT Desktop offices in Healey 3rd floor, room 009. If you have too many to transport to Healey, please request in your email to ask for Desktop team's help with transporting them. Note: If the steps above are not followed, IT Desktop team cannot accept the surplus computers.
