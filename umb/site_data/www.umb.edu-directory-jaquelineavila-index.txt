Department: Gerontology Title: Assistant Professor Send Email Area of Expertise Social determinants of older adult health, tobacco-related disparities among older adults, cross-national comparisons of health and aging Degrees PhD, Population Health Sciences, University of Texas Medical Branch Additional Information Dr. Jaqueline Contrera Avila is a Population health researcher with experience in U.S. and Latin America population-based data in the topics of health disparities, Hispanic aging, cognition and tobacco control. Before coming to UMass Boston, she was a postdoctoral fellow at Brown University School of Public Health. Her research program seeks to understand how tobacco exposure impacts cognitive decline in different countries and to intervene to reduce racial and ethnic disparities among older adults at high risk for lung cancer.
