Accounting & Finance Title: Associate Professor Location: McCormack Hall Floor 05 Phone: 617.287.3181 Send Email Biography Jay Lee is an Associate Professor of Accounting at University of Massachusetts Boston. His research focuses on how country-level institutions shape financial accounting and auditing and how financial reporting and audit quality impacts the valuation in capital markets. His work has been published in top-tier academic journals, including Review of Accounting Studies, Contemporary Accounting Research, Journal of International Business Studies, and Auditing: A Journal of Practice and Theory . Additional Information Background Before joining UMass Boston, Prof. Lee was an assistant professor at Hong Kong Baptist University. Prior to entering academia, he obtained his CPA in Korea and worked for PricewaterhouseCoopers and Deloitte in Korea as an auditor.
