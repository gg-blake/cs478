Counseling & School Psychology Title: Associate Professor Location: Wheatley Hall Floor 02 Phone: 617.287.4034 Send Email Degrees EdD, Boston University Additional Information Laura Hayden, EdD, is an Associate Professor and Co-Graduate Program Director of Counseling Psychology. Her research shows a commitment to the local urban community and larger global community, focusing primarily on positive youth development through sport and physical activity. She explores the relationship between physical activity and life skill development among at risk youth, with attention on understanding how youth develop culturally relevant skills that contribute to their success within their community. She examines the strength of interdisciplinary team approaches to student development, highlighting the importance of the counselor/athletic coach relationship.  A secondary line of inquiry considers the impact of mental skills and mental strength in high performance settings.
