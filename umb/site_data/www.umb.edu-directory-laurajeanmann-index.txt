Department: Nursing Title: Clinical Lecturer A Send Email Areas of Expertise Emergency/Urgent Care, Family Practice Degrees BS, Bridgewater State University ASN, Lawrence Memorial/Regis College BSN, UMass Boston MSN/FNP, Massachusetts College of Pharmacy and Health Sciences Additional Information Laura Jean Mann earned her Associate degree at Lawrence Memorial/Regis College in 2011, her Bachelor of Science in Nursing at the University of Massachusetts Boston in 2014, and her Master of Science in Nursing, where she became a Family Nurse Practitioner, at Massachusetts College of Pharmacy and Health Sciences in 2017.  In addition, Laura Jean also holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Management from Bridgewater State University.  As a registered nurse, Laura Jean started her career at Spaulding Rehabilitation Hospital, in Boston, followed by many years in the emergency department at Brockton Hospital.  As a FNP, she has worked in urgent and primary care, and continues to practice in urgent care.  She has served as a mentor for NP students studying at Simmons University and MCHPS.  In addition, Laura Jean is also certified in aesthetics, specializing in neurotoxins and dermal fillers.
