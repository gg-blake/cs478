We will host a team of 10 runners who will raise funds to support Camp Shriver at UMass Boston, an inclusive program for children with and without disabilities. Members of Team Camp Shriver will raise $3,500 to help low-income children with and without disabilities participate in a three season inclusive sports program. Together, we will ensure that all children, with and without disabilities, have an opportunity to learn, grow and have fun as equals. Applications for Team Camp Shriver for the 2024 TCS New York City Marathon are now open! Apply Members will receive: Guaranteed entry into the 2024 TCS New York City Marathon New York City Marathon swag, to be determined Training support provided by professional running coach, Rick Muhr . Marathon Team Handbook with training tips and fundraising support one-on-one phone calls / meetings with a development professional to help runners achieve their fundraising goals Camp Shriver running gear, and additional team meetings and events to be announced. We encourage you to find out more about Camp Shriver by watching our video and visiting our Facebook page. If you have any questions about the process, please contact: Barbara Gildea, Director of Development and Strategic Partnerships, by email or at 617.287.7250.
