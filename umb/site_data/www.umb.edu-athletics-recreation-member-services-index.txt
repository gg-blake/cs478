Recreation Connect Portal Locker-Rooms Located in the Beacon Fitness Center the locker-room facilities are available to all patrons who have a membership to Recreation. Lockers can be used on a daily basis during time of activity. Men's and women's locker rooms include showers and lockers. *NEW* Looking to lock your belongings up while you work out? We now offer free day locks at the fitness center. Please see the front desk attendant. Equipment checkout The Campus Recreation Center has a free equipment check-out service. Patrons can pick up equipment at the Welcome Desk. Equipment that is available to be checked out includes basketballs as well as racquetball, squash, badminton, ping pong, weight belts, other sport and fitness equipment. Contact If you need assistance you can go to the Member Services Station located in the Beacon Fitness Center.
