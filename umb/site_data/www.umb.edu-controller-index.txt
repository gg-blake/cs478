The Controller's Office is responsible for financial accounting and reporting, fiscal operations and audits, tax compliance, payments to vendors and business travel. Announcement: Tax year 2023 forms will be mailed to students soon. If you have received aid or income from the university, in accordance with IRS regulations, you may expect to receive a 1099, 1098-T, or 1042-S (non-resident alien). Please refer to the this Student Tax Form FAQs 2023 for additional information.  We recommend consulting with your tax preparer to understand how to handle this information.
