There are two primary categories of funding opportunities, external and internal . External funding opportunities are available through federal sponsors , state sponsors , and private foundations . To assist you in identifying external funding opportunities, the Office of Research and Sponsored Programs provides all members of the UMass Boston community with access to external funding opportunities databases .
