This page includes explanations, tools and referral resources for faculty who advise students with declared majors in the College of Liberal Arts. It is a supplement to the workshops, consultation, and communications from the CLA Advising office. Ana Frega, Director of CLA Advising, is available for all questions regarding this content and for advising support to faculty.
