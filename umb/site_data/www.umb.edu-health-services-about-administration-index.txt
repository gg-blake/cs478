Serving as the key liason between local and state health departments, UHS ensures compliance with student immunization requirements and oversees communicable disease exposure follow-up and control on campus. UHS Administrators play a pivotal role in shaping university-wide policies and advocating for the overall health and wellbeing of the campus community. To achieve these goals, UHS professional staff members hold positions on university-wide committees and task forces. The Executive Director provides expert advice and consultation to senior university leadership and stakeholders on various health-related issues impacting the campus environment.
