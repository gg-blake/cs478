Peer Recovery Meetings - Peer led online recovery meetings for students struggling with or recovering from substance use. Peers are SAFE Academy trained. Has meetings 3 times a week. Daily Collegiate Recovery Meeting - offers meetings from 8-9PM, peer-to-peer connections. Recovery Community Meeting - A weekly recovery meeting open to the whole UMB community, including staff Additional Resources Phoenix Live Streaming Fitness, Yoga, Meditation, & Recovery Support Meetings Phoenix Live Streaming Fitness, Yoga, Meditation, & Recovery Support Meetings - In partnership with Recovery@UMB, this service offers virtual services on a variety of fitness and meditation topics as well as support meetings targeted towards individuals recovering from substance use.
