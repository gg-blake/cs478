Anthropology Commencement Every May we have our own Anthropology graduation ceremony for majors and master’s students to celebrate their achievements with faculty, family, and friends. Please check here in the spring semester for announcements about the location, timing, and plans for such an event. To see how we celebrated in 2022, please download the program or watch a recording . To see how we managed in the “virtual” pandemic year, visit our YouTube channel for the celebrations of 2021 and 2020 .
