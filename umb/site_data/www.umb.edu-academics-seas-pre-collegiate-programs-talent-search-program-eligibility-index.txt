A student may apply if he or she meets all of the following requirements: a)  Is a U.S. citizen or permanent resident of the United States. b)  Is a potential first-generation college student (neither custodial parent has earned a four-year college degree) and/or is from an income-eligible family. c)  Is a student at one of the following schools: Jeremiah E. Burke High School , Charlestown High School , Dearborn STEM Academy , Excel High School , Madison Park Technical Vocational High School , McCormack Middle School or TechBoston Academy . d)  Is between the ages of 11 - 27 and has completed the fifth grade.
