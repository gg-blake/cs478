We take great pride in your success. By sharing your personal information and professional accomplishments you contribute to our growing alumni network, strengthen the visibility of UMass Boston, help us promote you in our publications, and gain special access to career development and other educational programs. To update your information with our alumni office, please contact alumni@umb.edu . Support the College Strengthen the Manning College of Nursing and Health Sciences teaching, learning, and research with a gift to the UMass Boston Fund. Your support helps us to adapt the curriculum to the changing demands of the industry, keep pace with constantly evolving technologies, and provide real world experiences for our students. To make a gift please visit our giving page or contact Casey (Gavin) McDonagh, Director of Annual Giving, at 617.287.5344 or Casey.McDonagh@umb.edu . Stay Involved There are many ways that you can remain connected and engaged with the Manning College of Nursing and Health Sciences at UMass Boston. Come for a campus tour, attend an alumni event , mentor a student, or present to one of our classes. For more information, please contact alumni.@umb.edu . Record Requests If you need assistance with getting records for your program completion, please call the Office of Student Success and Engagement at 617-287-7500 or cnhsstudentservices@umb.edu . Official University transcripts can be requested from the Office of the Registrar.
