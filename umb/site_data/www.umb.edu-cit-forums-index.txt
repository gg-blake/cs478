Forums consist of cross-disciplinary panel presentations and are open to all faculty, student, and staff members of the UMass Boston community. They provide opportunities for presenters and participants to share perspectives, engage in dialogue, and to collaborate across disciplines about critical and timely issues. Please contact CIT if you would like to suggest or participate in a forum. Teaching Workshops The Center for Innovative Teaching (CIT), in conjunction with the Office for Faculty Development , hosted two workshops for new faculty on September 18 and 19 so that they could learn more about teaching on our campus.
