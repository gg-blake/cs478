Cafe PATHS Engineering Career Cafe < Back To Calendar Thursday, October 3, 2024 Time: 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM Location: Campus Center, 1st floor, Terrace (1500) Organization: School for the Environment Partners Aligned to Heighten Broad Participation in STEM (PATHS) welcomes you to drop by our Engineering Career Cafe. Specialists and Industry professionals from the architecture, construction, and engineering fields will be on hand to showcase their jobs. You'll be able to ask questions and talk with them about their journeys. Lunch will be provided for those who participate.
