For any event held at the UMass Boston campus, the organization that is hosting the event is fully responsible for providing accessibility-related accommodations for the guests attending the event. If you have any ADA or accessibility-related questions or needs, please contact the organization that is hosting the event. Please note that the university does not provide wheelchairs, scooters, or other mobility devices. Accessible parking spaces are available for vehicles with official state disability plates or placards. People with disabilities who park in accessible parking spots are assessed the same fee to park as other drivers. Vehicles parked in accessible parking spaces without official disability plates or placards may be ticketed/towed. A map identifying accessible parking spaces at the UMass Boston campus is available at Getting Here .
