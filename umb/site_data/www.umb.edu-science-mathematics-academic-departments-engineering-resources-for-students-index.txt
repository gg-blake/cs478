As the economy continues to grow, so does the need for the latest technology and for engineers, the people who invent and apply it. Currently, there is a shortage of engineers nationwide. Thousands of engineering positions in Massachusetts alone are left unfilled, even though the average salary in the engineering profession is nearly twice that found in all other industries.
