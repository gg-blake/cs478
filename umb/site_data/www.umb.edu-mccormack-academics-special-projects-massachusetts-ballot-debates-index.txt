Live Conversation with Mayor Marty Walsh July 20, 2017 Media Coverage: YouTube , WBUR Live Conversation with City Councilor Tito Jackson July 19, 2017 Media Coverage: YouTube , Boston Globe , WBUR ELECTION 2016 As ballot questions often get little public notice, this series of debates was intended to raise the profile and further the discussion of the important ballot questions facing Massachusetts voters on November 8, 2016. All four questions are initiative petitions that would enact new laws. Each debate featured representatives on both sides of the ballot questions, as designated by the groups formally proposing or opposing each question.
