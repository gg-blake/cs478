Our assessment services realistically measure your skill levels in key areas. Testing saves you time and money by helping you choose the right courses and levels that will build on your current knowledge and skills. The Testing Center is located at the Campus Center, upper level, room 130, next to the One Stop Center. We offer a variety of testing services for new and continuing UMass Boston students, both degree and non–degree, and CLEP exam for college credit. Please review Testing Center policies before arriving. Mission Statement: Our mission is to provide accessible, comprehensive testing services for UMass Boston students and the community.  All tests are administered under standardized conditions that are efficient, fair, and secure.
