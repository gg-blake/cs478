where people exchange support and encouragement. Create a free NAMI account to join one. Contact your local NAMI affiliate to see what online and other resources are in your area. 7 Cups offers free online chat for emotional support and counseling. Also offers fee-for- service online therapy with a licensed mental health professional. Service/website also offered in Spanish. Additional Connections The Tribe Wellness Community The Tribe Wellness Community offers free, online peer support groups. Include focused groups: Addiction, Anxiety, Depression, HIV/AIDS, LGBT, Marriage/family, OCD and Teens.
