We: report a variety of official statistics on admissions, enrollment, retention, graduation, and HR (staff and faculty) coordinate and/or complete the official internal and external reports for the UMass Office of the President, the state (i.e. the Massachusetts Department of Higher Education), and the federal government (e.g., IPEDS) complete major college guide questionnaires, including the Common Data Set support UMass Boston’s strategic planning mission support institutional accreditation and program review (AQUAD) support departments and offices with their unit planning and mission conduct research focused on learning assessment, institutional effectiveness, accountability, and policy evaluation for the improvement of outcomes conduct national and institutional level student surveys work with campus constituents in the design of appropriate research strategies using a variety of techniques provide consultation on best practices for data collection and analysis, including survey methods participate in data sharing/exchange programs (e.g., Consortium for Student Retention Data Exchange (CSRDE) CIP Code Considerations Office of Institutional Research, Assessment, and Planning Quinn Administration Building, 3rd Floor, Room 13 617.287.5420 inst.research@umb.edu UMass 100 Morrissey Blvd. Boston, MA 02125 617.287.5000 Contact UMass Boston Directory Employment Civil Rights & Title IX Map Safety & Security Transportation Document Converter Website Requests Back To Top Instagram Facebook LinkedIn TikTok YouTube Copyright © University of Massachusetts Boston UMass System Accessibility Statement Privacy & Terms
