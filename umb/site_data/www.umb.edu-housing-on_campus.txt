Fall on-campus housing is for incoming first time, first year students. On-campus housing in the Spring will be available for undergraduate students, with priority being given to incoming first time, first year students, based on availability. **Please note, Housing is billed separately from your University charges and will not appear on your WISER account. It is strongly suggested that all residents have their own renter's insurance policy or see if you may be covered under your parent/guardian/guarantor's home owner's agreement.** Virtual Tours Take a virtual tour of our room types to start planning your journey as a Beacon. Why live on campus? Not only does living on campus provide a perfect first step for undergraduate students to live independently, you can also: Meet new people and start developing life-long friendships Get involved and broaden your perspectives Have an easy commute to class and activities: Be in class in minutes and in the middle of campus life Housing fees include your room, furniture, building amenities, utilities, and in-building events. Housing fees are separate from your UMass Boston bill and will not appear on your WISER account. Meal plans are required for all residents and will be on your University billing.
