Most of our computer labs are in the Healey Library building, Upper Level and 3 rd Floor.  IT Lab Operations also manages the Taylor Lab, 3 rd Floor of Wheatley. The IT Labs offer a variety of general use and specialized software taught by various departments and disciplines across campus.  Please check the lists to find the software you need, and for a more comprehensive chart of what software is installed in Healey Labs and other general use computer, see the PDF linked below.  If you have any questions, please email us at Computerlabs@umb.edu .
