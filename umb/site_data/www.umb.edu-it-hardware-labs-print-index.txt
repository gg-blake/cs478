Lab Student Printing at UMass Boston UMass Boston offers $15 of free print credits to enrolled students per semester, for use with our fleet of 18 student printing kiosks around campus and in the residence hall.  If you run out of free credits, you can pay for prints with BeaconBucks, credit card and other options. Pricing per page is: Black & White : $0.08  ($0.12 for double sided). Color : $0.35 ($0.34 for double sided).
