service and support to parents, guardians, and students by efficiently managing all student accounts through accurate and timely billing and refunding of tuition, fees, scholarship, and need-based aid and by developing and implementing tools and technologies to support easy access to information and flexible payment options. The Office also functions as the university Treasury accepting and processing payments on behalf of all university departments.
