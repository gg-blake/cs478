Information Dr. You completed his postdoctoral training in Gerontology and Geriatric Medicine at Wake Forest University School of Medicine (WFUSM), and was an Instructor of Medicine at WFUSM. He came to UMass Boston from the Department of Exercise and Nutrition Sciences, SUNY-Buffalo. His current research program focuses on the effects of mind-body exercise on multisite pain and falls in diverse older adults. Teaching Expertise Exercise Metabolism Exercise and Aging Research Methods Awards and Accomplishments Fellow, American College of Sports Medicine Fellow, The Obesity Society Dr. You's research group is currently accepting graduate (MS and PhD) students.
