U-Access Food Pantry Referral Form Do you need Emergency Funding for Housing? U-Access (Office of Urban and Off-Campus Support Services) has an Emergency Housing Fund through Liberty Mutual that is designed to help students during one-time housing emergencies by providing small grants (less than $1,000). The fund is reviewed by a committee of U-Access staff and is intended for students under the age of 24 who are homeless or housing insecure. You should submit any documentation of the housing emergency that you have and the more information, the better. Here is information and the application for the fund. Student Application Criteria Applicant is an undergraduate UMass Boston Student Applicant is currently enrolled in courses at UMass Boston Applicant is 24 years old or younger Applicant has not exceeded financial Aid limits due to federal regulations Applicant has not already been awarded Emergency Aid through Dean of Students office Applicant has not received Emergency Housing Aid within the current semester from U-Access Housing Aid Request UMass 100 Morrissey Blvd. Boston, MA 02125 617.287.5000 Contact UMass Boston Directory Employment Civil Rights & Title IX Map Safety & Security Transportation Document Converter Website Requests Back To Top Instagram Facebook LinkedIn TikTok YouTube Copyright © University of Massachusetts Boston UMass System Accessibility Statement Privacy & Terms
