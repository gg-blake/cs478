Additional Information Terry Halco is active as an accompanist, vocal coach, organist, and singer in the Boston area. Originally from Ohio, he received his musical training at Baldwin-Wallace College and at the New England Conservatory. Mr. Halco is the Minister of Music at Payson Park Congregational Church in Belmont, MA and is the accompanist for both the Chorus pro Musica in Boston and Musica Sacra. He has been the accompanist for the Emerson College Chorale and the Simmons College Chorale with which he toured England and Puerto Rico. He has also performed with the Tanglewood Festival Chorus and the vocal ensemble Capella Alamire. His recordings include Johnny Johnson directed by Joel Cohen with members of the Boston Camerata on the Erato label and The Early Josquin directed by Peter Urquhart on the Dorian label. At the University of Massachusetts Boston, Mr. Halco accompanies the UMass Boston Chamber Singers and the University Chorus.
