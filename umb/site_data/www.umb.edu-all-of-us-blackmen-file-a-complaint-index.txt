Please be aware that this does not constitute filing an official police report. To file an official police report, please contact the University of Massachusetts Boston Police Department at 617-287-1212 or in person at the Police Department, First Floor of the Quinn Administration Building. For incidents occurring off-campus in the City of Boston, please call 617-343-4730. IF THIS IS AN EMERGENCY, PLEASE CALL 911 or 617-287-1212 (UMass Boston Police Department) It is unlawful to retaliate against a student, employee or any other person affiliated with the University for reporting a concern, filing a complaint, or cooperating in an investigation of a complaint.
