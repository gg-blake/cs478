Demography of Aging Title: Professional - Hourly Send Email Degrees MS Management of Aging Services, University of Massachusetts Boston Additional Information Beth earned her MS, Management of Aging Services, from the McCormack Graduate School at UMass Boston. She volunteered for several years as a Council on Aging board member and worked as a project manager supporting the implementation of age friendly initiatives.  Her interests include intergenerational engagement; home modifications and community resources that support aging in place; and the impact of age friendly policy on health outcome.
