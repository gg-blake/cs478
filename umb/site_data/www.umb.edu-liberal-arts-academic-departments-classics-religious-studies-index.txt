Research & Centers Dean's Office Welcome to the Department of Classics & Religious Studies The Classics and Religious Studies Department offers undergraduate programs in Classical Studies and Classical Languages, and Religious Studies as well as several graduate degrees designed to prepare Latin teachers for secondary school teaching in Massachusetts, or anywhere in the U.S. Request Info Minor Graduate Programs Latin & Classical Humanities MA About Our Department The Classics and Religious Studies department annually hosts the Conventiculum Bostoniense, our week-long total immersion program in conversational Latin. Occasionally, we offer workshops for teachers on topics such as Latin pedagogy, conversational Latin, and the Advanced Placement (AP) tests.
