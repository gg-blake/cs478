Lockers are available for students to rent in the McCormack and Wheatley buildings. Students can rent a locker for a semester, for the academic year, or for the summer sessions. Students must provide their own lock and can only use a locker after renting through the Office of Student Activities and Leadership. **Lockers need to be cleaned out prior to the expiration of the rental contract** Costs: $20.00 per semester (September to December, January to May, and June to August) $25.00 for the academic year (September to May). Prices subject to change.
