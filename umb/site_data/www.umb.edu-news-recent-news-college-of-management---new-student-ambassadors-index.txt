New Student Ambassadors Menu menu Menu Recent News Publications 2022 2021 2020 Business & Management College of Management Announces New Cohort of Student Ambassadors 01/17/2024 |
    College of Management The College of Management team is thrilled to introduce eleven exceptional undergraduate students who will be representing the College as student ambassadors for the next year. These students were hand selected by Dean Venky Venkatachalam based on their outstanding leadership, dedication, and enthusiasm to make a positive impact. “I believe that this dynamic group of ambassadors will play a crucial role in fostering a sense of community, engagement, and collaboration ,” said Dean Venky Venkatachalam. “With a combined 10 unique spoken languages, their diverse skills, experiences, and perspectives will undoubtedly contribute to the success and vibrancy of our community.” Throughout their time as ambassadors, they will be representing the College at events along with interacting and mentoring current and prospective students. They will also be the voice of the student body. Their experiences, opinions, and ideas will help shape the future of our campus community and directly influence improvements within the College of Management. Congratulations to the next cohort of College of Management ambassadors!
