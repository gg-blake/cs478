Our program provides a comprehensive education where students learn foundation s for understanding consumers and effective marketing strategies, as well as the skills and knowledge required for the dynamic nature of the marketing discipline . Our courses are taught by faculty who are experts in their fields. Our curriculum is designed to build fundamental knowledge and broaden students’ perspectives in various areas of marketing, from P rinciples of M arketing , C onsumer B ehavior , and Service Marketing to Digital and Social Media Marketing , Marketing Analytics, and Sports Marketing . Their research has appeared in top marketing journals , including Journal of Business Ethics, Journal of Marketing, Journal of Marketing Research, Journal of Service Research, and Marketing Science.
