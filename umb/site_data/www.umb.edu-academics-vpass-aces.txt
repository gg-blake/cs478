Here at the Academic & Career Engagement & Success (ACES) Center, we’re focused on supporting and empowering students and alumni throughout their academic and career journeys. We offer comprehensive resources, personalized guidance, and innovative programs that help develop career-ready skills, aid in career exploration, and inspire lifelong learning. And, we do it all by fostering an inclusive and equitable community where students and alumni from diverse backgrounds can thrive academically and discover their unique talents. Through strategic partnerships with employers and our passion for excellence, we’re committed to helping all Beacons confidently navigate their path to a successful and fulfilling career. Get To Know Our Career Services Discover our services and programs for academic success and career readiness. Academic Advising & Course Registration For academic advising, we only see “Undeclared” undergraduate students who are enrolled: in the College of Liberal Arts in the College of Science and Mathematics in the College of Management who have less than 60 credits If these qualifications apply to you, book an appointment with an Academic Advisor. If you have already declared your major or are a student in the College of Management with over 60 credits, you should meet with an advisor in your respective college or program; please find information on scheduling an appointment at https://www.umb.edu/academics/advising/ . Career Success Advising For career success advising, we see all students—regardless of your major.
