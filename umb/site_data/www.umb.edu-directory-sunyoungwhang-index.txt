Additional Information Courses Taught Relational Databases, Database Management, Computer-based Information Systems, SAP ERP Systems Background Dr. Sun Young (Sunny) Whang studies institutional and identity work in the Metaverse at both organizational and individual levels. Her recent scholarship focuses on qualitative ethnography to explore social phenomenons in the newly emerging virtual worlds. She earned her bachelor's degree in Economics and her Master of International Studies degree in International Trade and Finance. Prior to pursuing her doctoral studies, she created and operated the KUBS Worldwide Business Research Rankings at the Center for Business Research Analytics of Korea University Business School. She also was a programme manager running KOICA government training programs on LDC's economic development at a private research and consulting institute.
