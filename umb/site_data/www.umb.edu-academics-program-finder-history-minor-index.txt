Minor Declare or Change Your Minor Request Info About UMass Boston Undergraduate Admissions Explore History’s Impact on Modern Societies. Through UMass Boston’s History Minor, you’ll explore the past and learn about a diverse range of historical topics. This program hones your analytical and critical thinking abilities and gives you a strong understanding of historical events and forces that have shaped human society. Coursework in this program focuses on interpreting primary and secondary sources, dissecting complex historical arguments, and clearly presenting your ideas in both written and oral forms. A History Minor gives you versatility across different career fields and majors. UMass Boston History alums have pursued careers in management, education, international humanitarian work, library science, public policy, and more. Expand Your Studies How to Apply/Declare Current Undergraduate Students: Declare or change your minor by completing an Undergraduate Program Add/Change form . Prospective Undergraduate Students: Apply for admission to UMass Boston by completing an application. Start by reviewing the first-year and transfer application pages for important information about requirements, deadlines, and application status checks. It explores how people have structured & managed their relationships with one another; how and why those structures and relationships changed over time; and how globalization has shaped life, identity, opportunity, and justice. Studying history deepens our understanding of the human condition and of how the past continues to shape our lives today.
