Professional Publications & Contributions Link to PubMed Articles Additional Information Dr. Wright’s program of research focuses on e-Health (computer-assisted) self-care interventions for individuals across the lifespan. Her expertise is in designing behavior change interventions that are theory driven, tailored, and delivered at home and/or connected to the primary care setting. Dr. Wright has studied scalable solutions for healthy weight management including tailored print-, Web-, and interactive voice response system (IVR). She also has expertise in the development of measures used to assess transtheoretical model constructs.
