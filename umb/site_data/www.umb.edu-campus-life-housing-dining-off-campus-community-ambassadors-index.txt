CAs promote opportunities for Beacons to become engaged members of the UMass Boston community while residing off-campus. CAs serve as a primary contact for students who commute to school from a family home (commuter) or nearby apartment (off campus) in the communities surrounding UMass Boston.  CAs communicate with students and family members through office interactions, phone calls, emails, and social media.  CAs encourage participation in campus, neighborhood, and community activities and help increase student awareness of both campus and community-based resources.  Commuter Assistants are supervised by the Assistant Director.
