Vecchione Department: Gerontology Institute Title: Consultant Send Email Areas of Expertise Connie Vecchione retired in 2019 as chief bar counsel at the Office of Bar Counsel of the Massachusetts Board of Bar Overseers, the attorney disciplinary agency. She had been an attorney with the Office of Bar Counsel since 1980, the last 12 years as chief counsel. She is a 1970 graduate of Brandeis University and a 1974 graduate of Northeastern University School of Law. She was previously a law clerk with the Massachusetts Superior Court (1974-75) and in private practice (1975-80).
