Conference Inaugural Title IX for All Conference < Back To Calendar Monday, April 7, 2025 Time: 10:00 AM - 2:30 PM Location: Campus Center, 3rd floor, Ballroom, sections B & C together (3550BC) Organization: Dean of Students, Queer Student Center, Sports Leadership Administration SA, Women's Center Th Inaugural Title IX for All Conference is hosted by the UMass Boston's Committee on Title IX Prevention Education. The Committee is chaired by Brikitta Hairston, M.Sc.; who oversees Title IX Compliance for students. The purpose of this conference is to culminate an environment of students, faculty, and staff for the goal of highlighting the importance of Title IX in Higher Education. Workshops, breakout sessions, and panels will be designed to focus on the biggest student-facing areas on our campus: Athletics, Housing, Student Affairs, and Academics.
