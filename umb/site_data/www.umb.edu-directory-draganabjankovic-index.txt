Bolcic-Jankovic Department: Ctr for Survey Research Title: Senior Research Fellow Location: Healey Library Floor 10 Phone: 617.287.7200 Send Email Biography Dragana Bolcic-Jankovic, PhD joined CSR in 2002. Since joining the center she has been involved in managing and conducting survey research projects.   She also serves as the Director of Survey Operations and is responsible for the oversight and operational control of all CSR data collection operations. Area of Expertise Working with collaborators on developing research proposals, questionnaire development, pre-survey evaluation of questions, managing data collection, and analyzing data for a range of studies focused on medical and health services research, ethics research in medicine and science, physician surveys, statewide surveys of schools on key health and risk behaviors, etc. Degrees PhD in public policy from the University of Massachusetts Boston MA in applied sociology and MS in public policy from the University of Massachusetts Boston BA in sociology from the University of Belgrade, Serbia Additional Information Dragana Bolcic-Jankovic, PhD, has taught a research methods course and guest lectured number of courses in the Departments of Sociology and Public Policy at the University of Massachusetts Boston.
