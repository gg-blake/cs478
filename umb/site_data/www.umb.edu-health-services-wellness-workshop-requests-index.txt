Guidelines for Requesting a Workshop Ensure a minimum of 5 attendees. Request must be made at least 1 week in advance. Choose a session duration: 15, 30, 45, or 60 minutes. Reserve appropriate space or coordinate with UHS staff. Workshop Offerings Incentives (such as food/beverages or freebies) are recommended but not required. General Medicine Services 101 Discover University Health Services' General Medicine clinic, services offered, and FAQs to understand our practices better. Presented by Chloe, Health Educator. Clear Voices, Strong Connections: Navigating Healthy Communication Let's find ways to enhance our interpersonal skills and promote constructive dialogue together. Explore practical strategies for effective communication that foster understanding, collaboration, and positive connections in both personal and professional spheres. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. Counseling Center 101 Insights into University Health Services' Counseling Center, services provided, and common queries answered. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. Embracing You: A Journey to Body Positivity Get ready to embark on a joyful journey of self-love and acceptance. We'll explore empowering strategies and positive perspectives to foster a deep sense of body positivity. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. Health-Related Trivia (choose your topic) Engage in health-related trivia on topics like alcohol/drugs, sexual health, specific awarenesses, and more. Prizes optional. Presented by Chloe or Beatriz (depending on topic). KORU Mindfulness Series KORU Mindfulness 101: Introduction to Koru Mindfulness and its benefits for emerging adults. KORU Mindfulness Drop-In: Experience mindfulness practices suitable for regular use and community building. KORU Mindfulness 4-Week Course: Dive deeper into mindfulness with a structured four-session course. Presented by Chloe or Beatriz. Let's Talk about Sex! Interactive program covering sexual health education and trivia on various related topics. Prizes optional. Presented by Chloe, Health Educator. Prescribing Self-Care: A Pre-Med Students Guide to Mental Health Are you a pre-medical student, overwhelmed by your course load and pressures of the STEM student's undergraduate life? This workshop helps put things into perspective. Learn how to use important self-care tricks, and how to make your mental health a priority. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. Put Your Records On: Music and Wellbeing Unleash the power of melody to enhance your mental and emotional well-being. Where beats meet your bliss, join us as we explore the therapeutic harmony between music and self-care for a truly uplifting experience. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. sExpress On-the-Go Want to offer your group a wide range of safer sex supplies? UHS staff bring their expertise in sexual health to answer all questions related to safer sex, STI testing, contraceptive use, lube, and more. We will bring a variety of safer sex supplies for all types of bodies and types of sex, including external condoms, internal condoms, oral dams, and water-based lube. Presented by Chloe, Health Educator. Stress Busting Self-Care Join us for a dynamic and informative session where you'll learn playful stress-busting techniques, embrace mindfulness, and discover practical stress management hacks to turn your stress into good vibes at our self-care stress management workshop. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. Time Well Spent: The Clockwork of Self-Care Navigate the chaos of life with our self-care through our time management workshop. Gain practical insights and tools to reclaim control over your schedule and prioritize self-care amid daily demands. Presented by Beatriz, Mental Health Educator. Submit a UHS Workshop Request Form!
