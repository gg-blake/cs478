The course emphasized clarity and coherence in case analyses, and focuses on professionalism within written business communication. Students work on appropriate delivery of information via a range of business letters, resumes, and cover letters. Notes: It is required of all students in the College of Management. Pre Requisites: Pre-req: ENGL 102, score of 1 or 2 BC on Writing Assessment or grade of B- or better in BC 230 and 30 or more credits.
