Match Deadlines and Meetings The IRB full board is scheduled to convene every month (only if needed). *These deadlines do NOT apply to exempt or expedited submissions which are instead reviewed by a designated IRB member on a rolling basis. Most UMB IRB submissions do not require review at a convened IRB meeting and are instead triaged in order of receipt for non-committee review. Therefore, it is strongly recommended that PIs submit as soon as applications are ready and not wait until the full board deadline date.
