City of Boston panel on Confronting Colonialism - Indigenous leaders and artists spoke about their work in the public realm in this three-part series. They addressed how symbols perpetuating colonial myths affect the lives of Indigenous people in the City and how these symbols contribute to the public health emergency of racism. Massachusetts Center for Native American Awareness (MCNAA) - A non-profit organization that uplifts and provides cultural and spiritual awareness for Indigenous persons in Massachusetts. They offer high-quality enrichment programs, lectures, and assistance in education and social change sectors.
