UMass Boston IT Licensed Software We can offer specially priced (site licensed) software to active faculty and staff members, as well as teaching and research assistants, to be used on either university-owned computers or for university business. Retirees from the university are not eligible for software. We do not sell software to undergraduate students, but we do have a number of titles available for free or at a discount. See: Discounts for Students Discounts for Faculty and Staff Purchase Software Visit our OntheHub software webstore: https://umb.onthehub.com/ You can purchase with PeopleSoft Finance SpeedType number or credit card. Licensing Responsibility Each employee is individually responsible for reading, understanding, and following all applicable licenses, notices, contracts, and agreements for software that he or she uses or seeks to use on company computers. Unless otherwise provided in the applicable license, notice, contract, or agreement, any duplication of copyrighted software, except for backup and archival purposes, may be a violation of federal and state law. In addition to violating such laws, unauthorized duplication of software is a violation of the company’s Software/Hardware Policy.
