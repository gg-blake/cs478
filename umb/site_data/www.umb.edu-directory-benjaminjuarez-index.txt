Boston University, C.M. of Mexican Academy of History, Exec. Comm. of Latin American Gender Network at Yale, REI Council at Institute of Coaching, McLean Hospital. Taught at universities in US, Mexico and Europe. Latin Grammy Nominee, 1st Latin American to conduct in China. Recorded over 12 albums of Mexican cathedral music. Area of Expertise Latin American Studies Leadership studies Mexican Cultural History Mexican Music Additional Information He was director of Music and Dance at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), director of cultural activities at the Universidad Anáhuac Sur, General Director of the National Center for the Arts in Mexico.
