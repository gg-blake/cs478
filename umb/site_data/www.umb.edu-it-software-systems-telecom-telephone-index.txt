Transfer Calls to another Extension Pick up call on “Line 1”. If the call needs to be transferred Hit “Access 2 or Line 2” button Put in the 5 digit number/extension where you want to transfer the call, 7-XXXX Once you put in the phone number (5 digit extension), you may wait for the other party to pick up and notify them or ... Hit the "Transfer" button/key to transfer the call immediately without announcing the call. If the person you are transferring the call to is unavailable, the call will be diverted to his/her voicemail box. Conference Call Services Conference calls can be done on campus through the following procedures: 1. Desktop phone – Maximum of 3 parties Dial 1st party  (Include your assigned codes when dialing outside of 617 area code) Acknowledge your first party and then put them on “hold” Press “Access 2” button and dial 2nd party After 2nd party answers, announce yourself Press “Conference” button.  This will connect all parties. 2. Conference Bridge Calls  – Maximum of 20 parties Conference bridge calling is also available for a minimum of 8 but not more than 20. This service is provided by Verizon. A toll-free number with codes for the “Initiator” & “Participants” as well as a confirmation number will be emailed to the requestor for them to dial into. Please request this service ahead of time by sending an email to itservicedesk@umb.edu with the time, date, number of participants, and contact person, and Press 9 to connect all parties. Request for your own department conference bridge can be requested through itservicedesk@umb.edu. Telecom will assign your own conference bridge number, all calls are charged on a monthly basis and will be included in your monthly phone bill. *Please contact Telecom at 7-5010 or 617-287-5010, if you have other needs that are not listed here.
