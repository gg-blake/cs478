Monday Harbor Cruises < Back To Calendar Monday, October 21, 2024 Time: 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM Location: M/V Columbia Point Organization: Marine Operations, Student Affairs Join Us for a Harbor Cruise Enjoy a narrated cruise around Boston Harbor aboard the M/V Columbia Point. Dates: Monday July. 22, Aug 26 September 23, October 21 Time: 12:00 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. Location: Cruises depart from the Fox Point dock, located adjacent to University Drive between Wheatley and McCormack Halls. Tickets: Tickets can be purchased in advance or available on a first come, first-served basis at dock 30 min before boarding.
