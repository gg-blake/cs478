The Adaptive Computer Lab (ACL) helps faculty accommodate students with unique learning needs by offering training and advice in the use of adaptive technology (AT). Online Accessibility Online content can be challenging to navigate for students with disabilities.  We offer consulting and training to faculty to make online content accessible to all. The lab is available for class tours and presentations. Contact the ACL Coordinator matt.mccubbin@umb.edu to schedule an appointment. For Students Request a referral from the Ross Center . Then make an appointment with the ACL Coordinator for a tutorial.
