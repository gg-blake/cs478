Requests & Referrals Meeting Request Refer a student to our office Dean of Students Office staff is available to help you by email, phone, or online via video conference. Email : dean.students@umb.edu Phone : 617.287.5800 Please leave a message, including your name and phone number, and someone from the office will call you back during business hours. Request a Meeting or Phone Call : Meeting Request Form Please indicate on the form times you are available and if you need to meet outside of business hours. After submitting the form, a staff member will reach out to you according to the information you provided. Staff are available to meet in person, on Zoom/virtually or over the phone. Refer or report a student to our office : Maxient Referral Forms . Forms : Please review our forms page to request an excused absence or to submit a form or document to our office. Business Hours The office is open Monday through Friday from 8:30am-5:00pm.
