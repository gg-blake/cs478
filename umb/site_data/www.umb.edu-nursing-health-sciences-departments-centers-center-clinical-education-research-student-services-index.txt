Tailored tutoring sessions, lasting one hour, cover challenging course concepts, study strategies, test-taking proficiency, and essential organizational skills.  For clinical skills enhancement, undergraduate nursing students can schedule sessions to practice various clinical skills, including organization and prioritization of patient care, medication administration, clinical reasoning, concept map development, and application of nursing process phases to patient-centered care.  Individual and small group tutoring slots are available Monday to Friday, on a first-come, first-serve basis.
