Belong at UMass Boston Students starting their college journey for the first time will attend an overnight orientation experience. This two-day program is filled with important information to support your academic and student success. Housing accommodations in our on-campus residence halls are provided to all students on the first night of the orientation program. First-Year Student Orientation Learning Outcomes As a result of attending first-year student orientation, students will… understand UMass Boston’s values of being an anti-racist, health promoting campus demonstrate the ability to navigate campus academic technologies realize the transitional challenges they may encounter during the first year and how to manage them complete their course registration process through connections with academic advising begin to establish community connections with other first-year students learn how to engage outside the classroom through co-curricular activities First-Year Student Orientation menu First-Year Student Orientation Orientation Dates Pre-Orientation Checklist Frequently Asked Questions Steps to Register for Orientation Follow these steps below to select your orientation date: 1. Pay Your Deposit Pay your new student deposit on the Beacon Gateway. It can take up to 2-3 business days for your deposit to process. If you have questions about your deposit, please contact Undergraduate Admissions at (617) 287-6100 or undergrad.admissions@umb.edu . 2. Register for a Session Once your deposit has processed, return to the Beacon Gateway and click on the 'events' tab. Under the 'events' section you will see New Student Orientation. Click 'Find a Session' under New Student Orientation to select a date that works best for you. Complete the questionnaire and be sure to click SUBMIT to confirm your registration. 3. Check Your Email Once you register, a confirmation email will be sent to your application email and your new UMass Boston email address. Reminder emails with additional details will be sent prior to your orientation session. Please check your email regularly and review this important information. 4. Review the Checklist Prior to your Orientation, review the Pre-Orientation Checklist to prep for your session. If you have any questions, contact the orientation team at orientation@umb.edu . Orientation Facts 2300+ students attended First-Year Orientation in summer 2023 95% of students feel prepared to succeed at UMass Boston after orientation 87% of students feel a stronger sense of belonging at UMass Boston after orientation Pre-Orientation Checklist Once you have registered, please complete the following tasks before your orientation session. UMass Boston Email Set up your email account through the first-time login process Multi-Factor Authentication Set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), a security setting on all university login sites Assessment Testing Review testing requirements and complete any necessary tests so you can select your first semester classes at orientation Beacon Card Visit the Beacon Card portal to upload a photo and order your student ID for pickup at orientation Immunization Records Visit the My Health Beacon Portal to upload your immunization records for University Health Services to review Contact Us Questions? Please feel free to contact our office for assistance.
