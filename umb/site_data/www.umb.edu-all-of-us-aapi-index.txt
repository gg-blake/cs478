Resources to Uplift Asian & Asian American Voices UMass Boston is the third most diverse college campus in the U.S. and is committed to supporting and celebrating AAPI-identifying students. We're a designated Asian American and Native American Pacific Islander-Serving Institution (AANAPISI). That means we're committed to financial aid, academic opportunities, enhanced facilities, and support for AAPI and Indigenous American students. UMass Boston stands against any and all acts of hate against AAPI communities.
