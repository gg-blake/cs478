Provost's Office Trang Ho Memorial Scholarship The Trang Ho Memorial Scholarship The Trang Ho Memorial Scholarship is awarded each summer in honor and memory of Trang Ho, Urban Scholars class of 1992. One award of $500 is given to a graduating senior who displays leadership, commitment to the Urban Scholars program, and has been accepted into a four-year university.
