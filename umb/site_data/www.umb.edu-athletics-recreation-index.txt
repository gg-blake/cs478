Fun and Fitness Get ready to unleash your competitive spirit, stay active, and have a blast with our wide range of recreational opportunities. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or new to sports, we have something for everyone. Compete & Connect Participate in our intramural sports leagues and tournaments, where you can form teams with your friends or join as a free agent. From basketball and soccer to volleyball and ultimate frisbee, our intramural programs offer a chance to compete, make new friends, and enjoy friendly rivalry. If team sports aren't your thing, we've got you covered. Engage in our individual and group fitness classes designed to help you stay in shape, push your limits, and achieve your wellness goals. Join our invigorating yoga, Zumba, or boot camp classes, or make the most of our state-of-the-art fitness center equipped with top-notch cardio and strength training equipment. Shoot hoops, play table tennis, and more on a first-come, first-served basis. Our open recreation program offers a wide range of recreational activities for students, staff, and faculty. Join us for exciting recreational opportunities and stay active.
