Honors College - Dean's Office Title: Program & Advising Coordinator Location: Wheatley Hall Floor 04 Phone: 617.287.5501 Send Email Additional Information Jason Roush has worked in higher education for the past two decades, with experience at such institutions as Harvard University and Boston University. He previously taught at Emerson College, where he also administered and advised in the Honors Program for nine years. Roush’s academic background is in literature and creative writing. His poems and criticism have appeared widely, and he has published four collections of poetry. Originally from Cincinnati, Ohio, he’s been a proud resident of the Boston area since 1993.
