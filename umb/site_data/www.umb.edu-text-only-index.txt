SensusAccess is a self-service solution that automates the conversion of webpages and documents into a range of alternative formats, including Braille, mp3, and e-books for the visually and reading impaired. This service requires a UMass Boston email address. If you need help with SensusAccess or have questions, please contact rossaltformat@umb.edu or the Ross Center at 617.287.7430.
