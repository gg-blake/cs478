Here's how you can schedule an appointment and prepare for your visit: Who Can Be Seen at UHS? University Health Services provides comprehensive care for all UMass Boston students. Booking an Appointment My Health Beacon Portal: Conveniently book appointments online through your My Health Beacon Portal. Phone: Call us at (617) 287-5660 to speak with our friendly staff and schedule your visit. Emergency: In case of emergencies, always dial 911. Before Your Appointment COVID-19 Precautions: If you have COVID-like symptoms before your appointment, please take a COVID-19 test and call us at (617) 287-5660 before coming in. Insurance: Upload a copy of your insurance card to your My Health Beacon Portal before your appointment. Language Line Translation Services : If you do not speak or understand English, please request Language Line translation services to ensure effective communication during your appointment. To request, please call (617) 287-5660 to let the front desk know or notify the front desk/the provider upon arrival. After-Hours and 24/7 Care Medical Advice: When UHS is closed, our Nurse Triage Line at (617) 287-5660 offers 24/7 medical advice. Mental Health Crisis Support: Access 24/7 crisis support by calling (855) 634-4135 for immediate assistance. After-Hour Emergencies For more information or assistance, please contact us at (617) 287-5660. Your health and well-being are priorities at University Health Services, ane we're here to support you throughout your time at UMass Boston.
