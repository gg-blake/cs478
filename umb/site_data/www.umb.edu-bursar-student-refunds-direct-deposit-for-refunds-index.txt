8:30 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. Friday 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Direct Deposit for Refunds Announcements Once you set up direct deposit in WISER Student Self Service, any monies owed to you, such as refunds for over-payment or financial aid excess amounts, will be deposited directly into one bank account that you have designated. Think about the advantages: Quicker access to your money. No special trip to the Bursar’s Office to pick up your check. No waiting in line at the bank or ATM to deposit your check. No worrying about a lost check. Here’s what to do: Make sure you have your bank’s routing number and your checking or savings account number at hand. (Must Be a USA bank) Log into WISER Student Self Service, click on Campus Finances, then click on " Enroll " in Direct Deposit. Instructional video: How to enroll in direct deposit . Job Aids: How to Update/Cancel Your Direct Deposit Account.
