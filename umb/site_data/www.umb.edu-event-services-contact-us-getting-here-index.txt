Our scenic waterfront campus is adjacent to the John F. Kennedy Presidential Library and the Edward M. Kennedy Institute for the United States Senate . UMass Boston is easily accessible by public transportation . The university offers a free shuttle service between the campus and the MBTA JFK/UMass Station. For those who drive , we have several parking lots on and near the campus. Taking Public Transportation?
