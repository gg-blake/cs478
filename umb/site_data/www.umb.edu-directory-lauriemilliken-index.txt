Professional Publications & Contributions Laurie Milliken's research interests include the regulation of body composition in response to exercise and physical activity throughout the lifespan and methods of accurately measuring body composition. She is currently the CEO of the Global Health and Body Composition Institute which fosters body composition expertise worldwide. She is involved in body composition training and has co-authored several chapters in the book, ACSM's Body Composition Assessment which she also co-edited with Dr. Tim Lohman.
