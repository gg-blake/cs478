Events Thinking About Climate Change Conference Thinking About Climate Change Conference < Back To Calendar Friday, October 25, 2024 Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM Location: Campus Center, 3rd floor, Ballroom (3550), Campus Center, 3rd floor, Conference Room (3540), Campus Center, 3rd floor, Ballroom Hallway (3520) Organization: Art Department Thinking About Climate Change: Art, Science and Imagination in the 21st Century , a research conference sponsored by the Art and Art History Department, the University Hall Gallery, The Grossman Gallery, the Environmental Sciences Department, the Sustainable Solutions Lab, the College of Liberal Arts, the College of the Sciences, and the English Department at the University of Massachusetts Boston. This conference considers the impact of collaboration between the arts, humanities and sciences on the global understanding of climate change. Within wider cultural contexts, artistic and scientific practices are engaged in power politics through historical, contextual, institutional and discursive formations. This conference explores interdisciplinary connections, shared foundations, tensions between individual relationships and institutional structures, with a focus on fostering exploration, awareness, education, and actionable solutions. It also aims to inspire researchers and artists to direct their passion and skills towards meaningful efforts, both individual and collaborative, to stem global heating and for sustainable development within planetary boundaries. The conference will take place in Boston on the University of Massachusetts Boston campus and aims to create an atmosphere for engaged dialogue through plenary sessions, panels, screenings, events and artistic exhibitions.
