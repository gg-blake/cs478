Language and Literacy Journal, 14(3), 61-82 Additional Information Christian W. Chun is the author of three books; Power and Meaning Making in an EAP Classroom: Engaging with the Everyday (2015), The Discourses of Capitalism: Everyday Economists and the Production of Common Sense (2017), and A World without Capitalism? Alternative Discourses, Spaces, and Imaginaries (2021), and the editor of the volume, Applied Linguistics and Politics (2022). He is currently working on his next book, The Language of Our Politics for Cambridge University Press. Research Interests Discourse analysis, sociolinguistics, ethnography, class, race and identity, language and political economy, linguistic landscapes, urban studies, visual studies, Asian American studies.
